About Us

Cusp Property Valuers (Pty) Ltd T/A Cusp (Pty) Ltd is a property valuation consultancy firm that specialises in property valuations in South African, and the rest of Africa. The company specialises in commercial, industrial, agricultural, and specialised property valuations. The company undertakes valuations for financial reporting, investments, mergers and acquisitions, and deal advisory purposes, The company was founded in 2024 and continues to provide quality valuation services.

VISION & MISSION

Vision 

The company’s vision is to be a leading provider of accurate and reliable property valuations. Empowering our clients to make informed decisions through reliable valuation reports.
